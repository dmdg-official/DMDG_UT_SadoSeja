### [Scene 1] 오프닝 (뒤주)
> **Prompt:** A cinematic low angle shot of a giant traditional Korean wooden rice chest (Dwiju) sitting alone in the middle of a vast palace courtyard at night. Heavy rain is pouring down relentlessly. Lightning strikes, casting a sharp, bright backlight that reveals the terrifying silhouette of the wooden chest. The thick ropes tied around the chest sway slightly in the violent wind. Dark fantasy, Korean Sumukhwa ink wash style, ominous, photorealistic, 4k.

### [Scene 2] 광기 (미쳐가는 사도세자)
> **Prompt:** Handheld camera shake, extreme close-up of a Korean Crown Prince's face in a dark room. His hair is messy and his eyes are frantically darting around, out of focus. A flickering red candle casts eerie, dancing shadows (chiaroscuro) across his face. He is gripping the hilt of a sword with trembling hands. Psychological horror, intense tension, traditional Korean setting.

### [Scene 3] 선고 (영조의 칼)
> **Prompt:** Over-the-shoulder high angle shot from behind an old Joseon King. The King throws a long silver sword onto the ground. The King is heavily backlit by a powerful spotlight, turning his face into a terrifying, featureless black silhouette. On the ground below him, a younger Crown Prince is lying flat on his face, desperately begging and crying, hitting his head on the floor. Dramatic, dark moody atmosphere.

### [Scene 4] 죽음 (피투성이 손)
> **Prompt:** Extreme close-up on the narrow crack of a sealed wooden chest in the dark. A single, very thin beam of light shines through the crack. Suddenly, three bloody fingers poke out through the crack, trembling violently and scratching the air. Slowly, the fingers lose strength and drop down lifelessly. The beam of light fades to complete blackness. Tragic, hyper-detailed, slow and dramatic movement.

### [Scene 5] 아웃트로 (낙관)
> **Prompt:** A massive traditional red Korean seal (Dojang) forcefully stamping onto a pitch-black background. A strong camera shake upon impact. The red seal glows with a subtle neon light effect in the darkness. Black ink spreads inside the borders of the seal.
